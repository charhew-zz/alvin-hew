About

Hello, my name is Alvin Hew, and I love art!

I first began my artistic career in the game industry, working as an Art Generalist. During this time, I had various responsibilities such as creating concept art, 3D modeling, 3D texturing, animating, and developing game assets.

Over the years, I began to find myself increasingly interested in illustration and I eventually began taking on freelance illustration work. As a freelancer, I have painted images for storybooks, games, musical albums, and various independently owned projects.

I am currently working as Senior Game Artist at Prodigy developing an educational online math game for kids.

Art is a great passion of mine, and I have dedicated my life to contributing what I can to the world through my images.
